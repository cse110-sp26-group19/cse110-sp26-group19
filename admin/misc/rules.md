# Coding Wizards: Team Charter

## 1. Personal Goals
* As a team, we aim to become better web developers and work with a diverse group of individuals.
* We aim to sharpen our communication skills. By improving our communication skills our team will work more efficiently because we are able to coordinate work among different members of our team.
* Furthermore, we want to exceed the expectations of our peers and TAs on assigned projects.

## 2. Values, Mission, and Common Goals
### Collective Team Values
* Write clean, organized code
* Clear communication
* Quality over quantity
* Collaboration/equal distribution of work

### Conflict Resolution
* **Time scheduling conflict:** Avoid by clearly communicating deadlines well in advance (at least 3 days before due date).
* **Disagreements between two members:** Introduce a third person to make the final decision. Or use a random number generator.
* **Missing deadlines:** If possible, extend the deadline. Offer help to the person who missed the deadline. Identify what caused the deadline to be missed to avoid it happening again.
* **Address situations when rules aren't followed:**
    * 1st time: warning.
    * 2nd time: kept on record.
    * 3rd time: group intervention.
    * 4th time: TA involvement.
* **Communication Protocols:** If a member is not responding, contact team leads (Tybalt and Anand). Set up transparent communication protocols: Use Slack workspace channels. On M-F, members must respond within 24 hours.
